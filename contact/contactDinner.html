Dinner with Bea

An hour-long aperitif with Beatrice, or a two and a half hour dinner with her? If it is your wish, perhaps it will be possible to make it come true. It will be an elegant meeting, where you can ask Bea all your curiosities and you can talk openly about any topic. It doesn't matter whether you are a woman, a man, a couple or a group of friends: mind, dialogue and open-mindedness will be the basis for an unforgettable drink or dinner.

The cost of this experience is Euro 300 for an aperitif and Euro 500 for dinner. 20% of the amount will be donated to the Midori Association - Association Against Eating Disorders.

After filling out the form, wait for a contact via email or via WhatsApp where dates and locations will be proposed to you. Once the appointment has been confirmed, you will be asked to pay a deposit of 50 euros to confirm the experience.
